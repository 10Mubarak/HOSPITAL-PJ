About Mercywell

At Mercywell we combine modern medicine with compassionate care. Our mission is to deliver exceptional healthcare across all ages with dignity and respect.

Our History

Founded in 2005, Mercywell started as a small private clinic and has grown into a full-service private hospital with 150 beds, modern operating theaters, diagnostic imaging, and a dedicated emergency care unit.

Mission & Values

  • Patient-first care
  • Clinical excellence
  • Integrity and transparency
  • Continuous improvement
